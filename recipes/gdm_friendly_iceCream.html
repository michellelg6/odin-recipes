GDM Friendly Ice Cream

Diabetic, but cannot resist the ice cream? Not to worry! This ice cream is delicious, but also healthy and low carb!

Ingredients:

  • Stevia flavored chocolate
  • Low Carb Yogurt
  • Fruit or berries
  • A freezer
  • An open mind that food doesn't need to be unhealthy to be tasty

Steps:

  1. Freeze Yogurt
  2. Break and add chocolate
  3. Add fruit or berries
  4. Enjoy! :)
